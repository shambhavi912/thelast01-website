About Me

Writer, photographer, and creative storyteller.

Hello, I'm Shambhavi.

I'm a writer and photographer interested in stories about people, memory, and everyday moments. My work explores the quiet emotions that shape our lives and relationships.

Through writing, I explore narratives about identity, family, and personal transformation. Through photography, I capture fleeting moments and the subtle beauty of places and people.

This website is a space where I share my writing, photography, and creative projects.
